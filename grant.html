The InspireCS Grant

Want to learn with us, but don't have hardware at home?

We believe your opportunities shouldn't be limited by your socioeconomic status. If you need a computer to participate in our camp, we'll gladly lend you one of our twenty Dell Latitude laptops, funded generously by the No Starch Press Foundation.

To receive a laptop, simply fill out the form below, and we'll get in touch with you.

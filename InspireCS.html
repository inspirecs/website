Our Mission

Computer Science is one of the fastest growing and most profitable fields of study available today. But early exposure to the field is critical for such a dynamic and competitive subject, and many students are unable to break through, held back by the financial barrier of the costs of technology.

InspireCS hopes to change that, by providing low-cost, week-long camps in all fields of computer science for students in the Houston area, with all materials such as laptops and notebooks provided. Furthermore, our learning materials and resources are all open-source and freely available online.

Our Curriculum

Through our curriculum, students will be exposed to and work on projects in every field of computer science, such as hardware, desktop application development, web development, and database management. Where possible, the curriculum adheres to pre-existing standard guidelines, and assumes no prior knowledge. Start learning here.

Our Resources

InspireCS custom set of tools for learning, developed in-house, makes the computer science learning process easier and faster. From a custom operating system - InspireCS Linux - with all the development tools one needs provided out of the box, to online videos and textbooks, the whole set of supplementary materials allows one to learn the fundamentals of computer science entirely by themselves.
